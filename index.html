"Upgrade your Cue to Upgrade your Game!"

POP's Custom Cue Extensions is a family business running since 2018. We specialize in building custom cue extensions that aim providing several benefits to a player, depending on their preferences and playing style. Here are some ways a custom pool cue extension can help:

  • Increased Reach: Pool cue extensions can help players reach shots that are far away or difficult to reach with a standard cue. This can be particularly useful when playing shots that require stretching across the table.
  • Improved Stability: Some players find that using a cue extension improves the stability of their stroke, especially for shots where they need to apply power or precision.
  • Customization: Custom pool cue extensions can be tailored to the player's specifications in terms of length, weight, and material. This allows players to fine-tune their equipment to match their preferences and playing style.
  • Consistency: By using a custom cue extension, players can maintain consistency in their stroke mechanics, especially if they frequently play with extensions during practice and competition.
  • Versatility: Pool cue extensions can be easily attached and removed from the cue, providing players with the flexibility to adapt their equipment to different playing situations and table layouts.
Overall, a custom pool cue extension can help players improve their game by providing added reach, stability, customization options, consistency, and versatility on the pool table.
